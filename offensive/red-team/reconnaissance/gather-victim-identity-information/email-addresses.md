# Email Addresses

Adversaries may gather email addresses that can be used during targeting. Even if internal instances exist, organizations may have public-facing email infrastructure and addresses for employees.

* TheHarvester
* [Hunter.io](https://hunter.io/)
* [Phonebook](https://phonebook.cz/)
* [Clearbit Connect](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/clearbit-connect-free-ver/pmnhcgfcafcnkbengdcanjablaabjplo?hl=en)

We can also go through Linked In to gather information about employee in organization. Once we have obtained the format of the email.&#x20;
